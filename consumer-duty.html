CONSUMER DUTY POLICY

Our Commitment

At InsureSafeLine, we are fully committed to the FCA’s Consumer Duty principles. We believe that our customers should receive fair value, clear information, and the support they need to pursue their financial objectives.

Consumer Duty Principles

  • Consumer Principle: We act to deliver good outcomes for retail customers.
  • Cross-cutting Rules: We act in good faith, avoid causing foreseeable harm, and enable and support our customers to pursue their financial objectives.

Four Outcomes

We focus on delivering four key outcomes for our customers:

  • Products and Services: Our products are designed to meet the needs of our target customers and are distributed appropriately.
  • Price and Value: We ensure our products offer fair value, providing benefits that are reasonable relative to their cost.
  • Consumer Understanding: We provide clear, timely, and understandable information to help customers make informed decisions.
  • Consumer Support: We provide support that meets our customers' needs throughout the life of the product.

Vulnerable Customers

We recognize that some customers may be in vulnerable circumstances. We have processes in place to identify and support these customers to ensure they receive outcomes that are just as good as those for other customers.
