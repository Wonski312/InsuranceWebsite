Our Property Insurance Products

Protecting your property is essential—whether it’s your home, rental investment, or commercial premises. Our tailored insurance solutions help safeguard buildings, contents, and income against unexpected risks.

Commercial Occupied Property

Cover for active business premises, protecting buildings and contents against risks such as fire, flood, theft, and accidental damage.

Commercial Unoccupied Property

Specialist protection for vacant commercial properties, covering higher-risk exposures such as vandalism, damage, and unforeseen incidents.

Residential Occupied Property

Ideal for lived-in homes and rental properties, providing protection for buildings and contents against everyday risks.

Residential Unoccupied Property

Designed for empty residential properties, ensuring continued protection during vacant periods, renovations, or change of tenancy.

Personal Household

Comprehensive household insurance to protect your home and personal belongings, offering reassurance for you and your family.
