Why Travel Insurance is Essential

Travel insurance is essential to protect against unforeseen events while travelling in the UK or abroad. From emergency medical treatment and repatriation to trip cancellations and personal liability, the right cover helps reduce financial stress and disruption.
With rising healthcare costs overseas and frequent travel disruptions, comprehensive travel insurance ensures you receive help when you need it most.

Travel Insurance – Frequently Asked Questions

Travel insurance is strongly recommended for all trips, as it helps protect against unexpected events such as medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost baggage, and travel delays both in the UK and abroad.

Travel insurance typically includes cover for emergency medical treatment, trip cancellation or curtailment, lost or stolen baggage, personal liability, and 24/7 emergency assistance. Cover varies depending on the policy and insurer.

Travel insurance is not legally required; however, it is highly recommended, particularly when travelling abroad, due to the high cost of medical treatment and potential travel disruption.

Business travel insurance is available and can include cover for work-related travel, such as medical emergencies, delays, and business equipment, depending on the policy terms.
