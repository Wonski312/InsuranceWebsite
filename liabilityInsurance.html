Why Liability Insurance Matters

Liability insurance helps protect your business from claims arising from injury to third parties, property damage, employee incidents, or errors in professional advice. It ensures continuity, peace of mind, and financial security in challenging situations.

OUR LIABILITY COVER

Public Liability

Covers claims from third-party injury or property damage occurring in connection with your business operations. Essential for businesses interacting with the public.

Employers’ Liability

Legally required if you employ staff. Covers compensation claims from employees injured or made ill during work.

Product Liability

Protects your business from claims arising from faulty products causing injury or damage to customers or their property.

Professional Indemnity

Covers financial loss to clients due to negligence, mistakes, or professional advice errors. Ideal for consultants, architects, and service providers.

Directors & Officers

Protects company directors and officers from personal liability arising from decisions, breaches of duty, or regulatory investigations.

Environmental Liability

Covers costs of pollution or environmental damage caused by business activities, including clean-up costs and legal fees.
